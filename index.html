Capturing and Tracking fromTwitter Data.

A framework will be developed to capture emergent events in the information security domain from Twitter data. Recently, different types of malwares, bugs and phishing attacks are detected on the internet. The framework is expected to predict the novel attacking technique which can help users to prevent being infected.
